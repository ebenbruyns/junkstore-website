**This Week's Pro Tip:** Use the sliders menu on any installed game to update, repair, or verify your game files - all without leaving Game Mode!

*Note: This feature works in both versions of Junk Store.*

<h2 style="text-align: left !important; margin-left: 0;">The Problem</h2>

Every now and then we get questions about how to update games due to new patches, or how to add newly purchased DLC content to already downloaded games (i.e. Cyberpunk 2077). Don't waste time downloading games over and over again, instead use the <strong>Update, Repair or Verify</strong> functionality built into Junk Store without leaving Game Mode or needing to restart your Steam Deck.

**Common scenarios when you may need to use this:**
- Newly purchased DLC content isn't showing up in-game
- New patch released for one of your games - Rocket League, Fall Guys, ZZZ, Arknights: Enfield, etc.
- During download some game files got corrupted
- Game crashing on launch that was working fine

<h2 style="text-align: left !important; margin-left: 0;">Where you can find these functions:</h2>

<ol style="padding-left: 1.2em; margin-left: 0; text-align: justify;">
<li>Go to your installed game in Junk Store</li>
<li>Open the game details</li>
<li><strong>Select the sliders menu</strong> (three lines icon)</li>
</ol>

![Sliders menu on game screen](/assets/images/blog/VerifyRepairUpdate/gamescreensliders.webp)

<ol start="4" style="padding-left: 1.2em; margin-left: 0; text-align: justify;">
<li>Pick <strong>Update</strong>, <strong>Repair</strong>, or <strong>Verify</strong></li>
</ol>

![Update, Repair and Verify options](/assets/images/blog/VerifyRepairUpdate/verifyrepairupdate.webp)

<h2 style="text-align: left !important; margin-left: 0;">What each function does:</h2>

### Update

Self explanatory but just in case...this will download updates only or new DLC content to save you downloading the game over and over again.

### Verify

Sometimes during download things can go wonky. This will verify downloaded files against the game's manifest to check if anything is missing or corrupted.

### Repair

If any files are missing or corrupted this will download those files causing issues and preventing the game from launching.
