Kia ora Everyone!

We are heading into King's Birthday weekend here in New Zealand. It means we get a public holiday on Monday (long weekend fun)! For us on the boat it doesn't mean much, we work 7 days no matter what day of the week. That's all good though, we enjoy what we do and most importantly it keeps us outta trouble.

Other than working, we haven't gotten up to much as usual. We did go for a meal at the boat club with one of our mates on the weekend, which was good fun and a much-needed break from the boat. Funny thing is, after we got dropped off we went back to work! All good though, we've got some boat jobs and chores coming up soon, so that'll force us to take a break.

As always we hope you are all having a fantastic week wherever you are in the world! This week's games were fun to test, so if you haven't already, make sure you give them a go yourself!
