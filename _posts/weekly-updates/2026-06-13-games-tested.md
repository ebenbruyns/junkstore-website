Kia ora Everyone!

This week's round of giveaways was a lot of fun to test. A mix of nostalgia and my fave genre made for some longer than intended 'testing' sessions. While I was busy testing, Eben was busy making updates to the File Manager and testing the new support system in the Portal. To be honest, I'm not sure who was having more fun.

I couldn't get one of the Amazon giveaway games, Sin Slayers: Reign of The 8th, to run. From what I could find online this is a known issue, I just couldn't track down the fix this weekend. I'll keep looking and may even get Eben to help work out what's going on, then report back.

Less than a month now before we go and house/cat sit for friends, so need to tear ourselves away from work to do some things on the boat. I'll need to prod Eben otherwise he'll just keep working and we'll run out of time. He hates it when I have to make him take a break to do something else, ah well guess someone has to be the bad guy sometimes.
