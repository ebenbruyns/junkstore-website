Kia ora Everyone!

What a weekend! The whole North Island was in the crosshairs of a cyclone, with states of emergency declared up and down the coast. Luckily it shifted east as it got closer, so the impact wasn't as bad as predicted - though there's still a massive clean-up happening where flooding and winds hit hardest.

We prepped the boat to ride it out: tied everything down, took our solar panels off, and stopped up any potential leaks. Then we waited. Thankfully it passed us by without much disturbance - a bit of rain and a short burst of high winds, but our boat and the island we're moored at came through pretty unscathed.

Had to conserve power with the solar panels down, which is why this one's a bit late. Did manage to test the giveaways from the weekend though!
