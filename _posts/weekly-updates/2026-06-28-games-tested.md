Kia ora Everyone!

What a week! Could do with getting back to more chill, quiet ones, what with the unexpected mooring move late at night in less than ideal weather. Couple that with needing to manage our extremely low supply of LPG (our only way to cook and heat water up on the boat), and it was a pretty stressful week. To top it all off, I had to make a supply run to the city for LPG and, just as important, coffee beans! I also grabbed a top-up of groceries and petrol (for our generator) to see us through the next two weeks until we go house/cat sitting for friends.

I had just enough energy today to test this week's giveaways, and there were some pretty good games in the mix! I've also made sure to find time this week, as has Eben, to actually play some games from our backlogs. Eben is still making his way through Cyberpunk 2077, and I'm switching between Freshly Frosted from Epic (started over a year ago) and Necroworm on Steam (can't remember when I started that one). Both are very similar puzzle games, so I'm happy to add this week's Voidwrought to the mix for a break.

The one I couldn't get sorted this week was Space Grunts. I tried it on both versions of Junk Store, a few different GE-Proton versions and a couple of different exes, but no luck so far. I'll keep tinkering to work out what it's missing, and might rope Eben in to help. If we crack it, I'll be sure to pop it in a future post.

Anyways, here's this week's round-up of games. Hope you all can find something that might make your playlist.
