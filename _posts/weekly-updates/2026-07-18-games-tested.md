Kia ora Everyone!

We're at the end of our first week down in Tauranga, getting our fill of land life! We're still trying to settle into some sort of routine, and it's been a bit of a challenge. We're so used to a small space, going to bed and getting up with the sun, and definitely not used to being this close to the shops! We've been getting up earlier and staying up later than we should, mostly because we can just flick a light on now without worrying about recharging the batteries.

Eben has set up his work space and barely leaves it. He's got a lot he wants to cram in before we head back to the boat. I've got my own list of jobs to work through too, so I'm busy sorting out what to tackle first while we're here.

Just a heads up: I couldn't get Framed Collection (GOG) working from this week's Amazon Prime giveaway. I'll rope Eben in to help me diagnose what's going on and report back.
