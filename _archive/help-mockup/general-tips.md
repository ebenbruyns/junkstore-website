      <h3>🔋 Extend Battery Life</h3>
      <p>Lower the refresh rate to 40Hz and cap frame rate to 40fps for most games. This can double your battery life while maintaining smooth gameplay.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="tip-card">
      <h3>🎮 Perfect Controls</h3>
      <p>Use Steam Input templates as starting points, then customize. Don't forget to enable gyro for fine aiming in shooters - it's a game changer!</p>
    </div>

    <div class="tip-card">
      <h3>📂 Organize Your Library</h3>
