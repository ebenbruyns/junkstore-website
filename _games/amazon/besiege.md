Besiege is a physics building game about constructing mechanical machines to annihilate armies, obliterate castles, and overcome obstacles.
* Create catapults, tanks, helicopters - anything you can imagine - with Besiege's intuitive and flexible building system and extensive 70+ block collection!
* Conquer your way through a single-player campaign of 55 destructible levels spanning across four island nations, each with its own environment.

**The Splintered Sea DLC**
* Dive into The Splintered Sea expansion, introducing water to Besiege for more machine-building conquests! Battle your way through a 15-level oceanic campaign, explore an all-new sandbox, and harness 8 new blocks to unleash chaos on the high seas.
* Following your relentless campaign from Ipsilon to Krolmar, take to the Splintered Sea, where people known as "The Offlanders" occupy a series of islands. Annihilate Admiral Drasckar's fleet of cutters and go toe-to-toe with his prized galleon. Slip beneath the waves, evading the Offlander's traps and fending off creatures of the deep. Obliterate Drasckar's fortress and unleash his horrifying nemesis!
* The Splintered Sea expansion comes with its own sandbox for you to explore. Sail through a rugged archipelago, challenge a mighty galleon, and terrorize local fishermen. Drop beneath the waves and explore the ancient ruins, or put your machine to the test on the water course.
