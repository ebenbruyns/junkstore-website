Survival: Fountain of Youth is a challenging single-player open-world survival game set on the Caribbean islands. Survive as a shipwrecked 16th-century explorer, craft tools and shelter, and uncover the secrets of both an ancient civilization and your own fate.

You are a member of Juan Ponce de Leon's expedition in search of the Fountain of Youth, based on an actual historic event of the Age of Discovery. After surviving a shipwreck, you need to learn to live on an uninhabited island. Hunt, gather and manage resources, build shelters, discover new technologies, develop skills, resist diseases, cope with weather conditions, fight predators, and much more. Your main goal is to survive. Your secondary goals are to figure out the fate of an extinct civilization, look for your shipwrecked crew, explore the islands, and maybe find the Fountain of Youth.
