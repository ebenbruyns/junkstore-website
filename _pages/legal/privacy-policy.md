<section class="js-section">
  <h2 class="js-section-header">What We Track (But Don't Collect Personally)</h2>
  <p>This website does not collect or store personal information directly. With your consent, we use third-party services to track anonymous usage patterns.</p>

  <h3>Google Analytics 4</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Which pages you visit and how long you stay</li>
    <li>What device/browser you're using (e.g., "Chrome on Windows")</li>
    <li>General location (country/city level, not your address)</li>
    <li>How you found our site (search, direct link, etc.)</li>
    <li>Anonymous visitor behavior patterns (IP addresses anonymized)</li>
  </ul>

  <h3>Google Tag Manager</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Enhanced analytics data collection and processing</li>
    <li>Custom event tracking (form submissions, downloads, etc.)</li>
    <li>Conversion tracking for business optimization</li>
  </ul>

  <p><strong>Important:</strong> We never see your name, email, or any personally identifiable information. The tracking data helps us understand what content is useful.</p>
</section>
