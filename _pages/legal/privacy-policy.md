<div class="policy-section">
<h3>What We Track (But Don't Collect Personally)</h3>
<p>This website does not collect or store personal information directly. With your consent, we use third-party services to track anonymous usage patterns.</p>
</div>

<div class="policy-section">
<h4>Google Analytics 4</h4>
<ul>
<li>Which pages you visit and how long you stay</li>
<li>What device/browser you're using (e.g., "Chrome on Windows")</li>
<li>General location (country/city level, not your address)</li>
<li>How you found our site (search, direct link, etc.)</li>
<li>Anonymous visitor behavior patterns (IP addresses anonymized)</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="policy-section">
<h4>Google Tag Manager</h4>
<ul>
<li>Enhanced analytics data collection and processing</li>
<li>Custom event tracking (form submissions, downloads, etc.)</li>
<li>Conversion tracking for business optimization</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="policy-note">
<strong>Important:</strong> We never see your name, email, or any personally identifiable information. The tracking data helps us understand what content is useful.
</div>

<div class="policy-section">
<h3>Your Rights & Choices</h3>
<p><strong>Cookie Consent:</strong> Use our cookie banner to accept or decline all tracking.</p>
<p><strong>Opt-Out Anytime:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout">Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on</a></li>
<li><a href="https://adssettings.google.com/">Google Ad Settings & Tag Manager</a></li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="policy-section">
<h3>Data Requests</h3>
