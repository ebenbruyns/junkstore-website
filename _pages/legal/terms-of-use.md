<div class="policy-section">
<h3>What You CAN Do</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Share links</strong> to our content freely</li>
<li><strong>Quote short excerpts</strong> (a paragraph or two) with clear attribution and a link back to the original</li>
<li><strong>Reference our guides</strong> in your own original content, with attribution</li>
<li><strong>Use screenshots</strong> for review or commentary purposes with attribution</li>
<li><strong>Embed</strong> our content where embedding is provided</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="policy-section">
<h3>What You CANNOT Do</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Copy entire articles, tutorials, or guides</strong> and republish them, even with modifications or restyling</li>
<li><strong>Present our content as your own work</strong></li>
<li><strong>Use our images without attribution</strong> or in a way that implies you created them</li>
<li><strong>Scrape or bulk-download</strong> our content for republication</li>
<li><strong>Use our content commercially</strong> without written permission</li>
<li><strong>Remove or obscure</strong> any copyright notices or watermarks</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="policy-note">
<strong>Attribution Example:</strong> "Source: <a href="https://www.junkstore.xyz">Junk Store</a>" or "Originally published at <a href="https://www.junkstore.xyz">junkstore.xyz</a>" with a direct link to the original content.
</div>

<div class="policy-section">
<h3>Image Usage</h3>
<p>Our screenshots, diagrams, and original artwork may be used for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Editorial/news coverage with attribution</li>
<li>Reviews and commentary (fair use)</li>
<li>Educational purposes with attribution</li>
</ul>
<p>Images may NOT be used for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Commercial purposes without permission</li>
<li>Implying endorsement or affiliation</li>
<li>Resale or redistribution</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="policy-section">
<h3>Tutorial and Guide Content</h3>
<p>Our installation guides, troubleshooting articles, and technical tutorials represent significant original work. These may not be:</p>
<ul>
<li>Republished in full on other websites or blogs</li>
<li>Copied and "restyled" as original content</li>
<li>Used as the basis for derivative guides without attribution</li>
</ul>
<p>If you want to create similar content, write your own original guide. You may reference our work with proper attribution.</p>
</div>

<div class="policy-section">
<h3>Reporting Infringement</h3>
<p>If you find our content being used without permission, please let us know:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.reddit.com/r/JunkStore">r/JunkStore on Reddit</a></li>
<li><a href="https://discord.gg/6mRUhR6Teh">Junk Store Discord</a></li>
</ul>
<p>We take content theft seriously and will pursue DMCA takedowns where appropriate.</p>
</div>

<div class="policy-section">
<h3>Permission Requests</h3>
<p>Want to use our content beyond what's permitted above? Contact us through our community channels. We're generally happy to grant permission for legitimate uses with proper attribution.</p>

<h3>Changes to These Terms</h3>
